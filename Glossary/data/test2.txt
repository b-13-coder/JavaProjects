communication
the act or process of conveying information or ideas, especially through language

lexicon
a complete set of words or vocabulary used in a particular language or field of study

morphology
the study of the structure and forms of words, including affixes, roots, and inflections

semantics
the study of meaning in language, including the meaning of words, phrases, and sentences

syntax
the set of rules governing the structure and arrangement of words in a language

glossary
a list of difficult or specialized terms, with their definitions, usually near the end of a book

literature
written works, especially those of artistic or intellectual value, including books, poems, plays, and essays
